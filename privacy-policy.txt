PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: December 2024

ZOOM VIDEO DIRECT DOWNLOADER EXTENSION

1. OVERVIEW

Zoom Video Direct Downloader is a Chrome extension that extracts direct download links from Zoom recordings, events, and clips. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

2. DATA WE DO NOT COLLECT

We DO NOT:
- Collect or track personal information
- Send data to external servers or third parties
- Use cookies or tracking pixels
- Monitor your browsing habits
- Create user profiles
- Sell or share your data
- Collect telemetry or analytics

3. DATA STORED LOCALLY

All data is stored ONLY on your computer in Chrome's local storage:

Extraction History:
- Zoom recording URLs you extract
- Direct download links
- Recording titles and metadata
- Transcripts (if available)
- Timestamps of extractions

Extension Settings:
- Data retention preference (7/30/90 days or indefinitely)
- Your chosen settings and preferences

Extension Logs:
- Activity logs for troubleshooting
- Timestamped records of actions
- Error messages and status updates
- Limited to 10MB storage (auto-cleanup)

4. NO CLOUD STORAGE

All data remains on your device. We do NOT upload, transmit, or store anything to:
- Cloud servers
- External databases
- Third-party services
- Our servers

5. USER CONTROL

You have complete control:
- View all stored history in Settings
- Delete individual entries anytime
- Clear entire history with one click
- Clear all logs with one click
- Choose automatic deletion after 7/30/90 days
- All data persists only until you clear it

6. DATA DELETION

To delete your data:
1. Open extension Settings (gear icon)
2. Click "Clear All History" to remove extractions
3. Click "Clear Logs" to remove activity logs
4. Uninstall the extension to remove all data

5. WHAT HAPPENS WHEN YOU UNINSTALL

All data is permanently deleted:
- History cleared from Chrome storage
- Logs removed
- Settings reset
- No traces remain

7. SECURITY

Your local data is protected by:
- Chrome's storage security mechanisms
- Your computer's file system permissions
- No network transmission = no interception risk
- Browser-enforced isolation

8. ZOOM API DISCLAIMER

This extension:
- Accesses public Zoom API endpoints
- Does NOT store your Zoom password or credentials
- Does NOT authenticate with your Zoom account
- Works only with publicly accessible recordings
- Follows Zoom's published API documentation

9. THIRD PARTIES

We do NOT share data with:
- Google (beyond Chrome telemetry)
- Zoom (we extract only, no data sent back)
- Advertisers or marketers
- Any external services

- Maintain transparency about data practices

15. SPECIAL NOTE ABOUT EXTRACTED DATA

When you use this extension:

- We do NOT store any authentication tokens
- We do NOT access private/password-protected recordings
- We do NOT access your Zoom account information
- Download links are temporary (expire in 24-48 hours)

16. DATA RETENTION DEFAULTS

By default:
- History kept for 30 days
- You can change to 7, 90 days, or indefinitely
- Logs limited to 1000 entries or 10MB
- All deletions are permanent

17. BROWSER STORAGE

- Limited to user's local machine
